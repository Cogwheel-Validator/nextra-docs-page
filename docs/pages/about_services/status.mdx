# Status Page

Service page is available [here](https://status.cogwheel.zone)

![Status page](./assets/statusPage.webp)

The Status Page provides real-time information on the health status of
our services, indicating whether they are available or experiencing
issues. It serves as a hub for checking the operational status of our
services, whether they are running smoothly or temporarily halted. In case
of any service disruptions, you can always refer to this page for updates.
